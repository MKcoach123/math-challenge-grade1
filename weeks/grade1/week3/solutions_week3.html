Week 3 — Answers & Solutions

Grade 1 Math Challenge
Problem 1
What is the middle gnome's name?
Answer: Tim
The left gnome says “I am Nim,” so he is Nim. The right gnome says “I am not Tim,” and since Nim is already taken, he must be Jim. That leaves only Tim for the middle gnome.
Problem 2
Which key is the right key?
Answer: D
Look at the outline of the keyhole in the lock, then find the key whose shape matches it. The one that fits is D.
Problem 3
How many apples does Kira have left?
Answer: 10
Kira eats 5: 25 − 5 = 20. Then she gives 10 away: 20 − 10 = 10 apples left.
Problem 4
How long is Milana's pencil?
Answer: 5
Line up the pencil with the ruler and read where its tip ends — the pencil is 5 long.
Problem 5
What number is behind the blob?
Answer: 110
The blob hides a number in 180 − ? = 70. That's the same as 180 − 70 = ?, so the missing number is 110.
