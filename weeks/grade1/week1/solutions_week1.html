Week 1 — Answers & Solutions

Grade 1 Math Challenge
Problem 1
How many more bricks are in the left stack?
Answer: 5
The two stacks are built the same way — except the left stack has one extra row along the bottom. That extra row has 5 bricks, so the left stack has 5 more than the right.
Problem 2
How many goals did Omar score?
Answer: 11
Add the goals from both halves: 4 + 7 = 11.
Problem 3
Which stone do you move, and what is its weight?
Answer: 2
The left side is 6 + 2 + 3 = 11 and the right side is 4 + 3 = 7. Move the smallest stone, the 2, over to the right: now the left is 6 + 3 = 9 and the right is 4 + 3 + 2 = 9 — balanced!
Problem 4
What number goes in the square with the ?
Answer: 35
Each step adds 5: 15 → 20 → 25 → 30 → 35.
Problem 5
How many frogs balance one cat?
Answer: 2
2 cats balance 4 frogs. Half as many cats balance half as many frogs, so 1 cat balances 2 frogs.
