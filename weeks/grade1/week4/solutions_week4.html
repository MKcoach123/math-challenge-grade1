Week 4 — Answers & Solutions

Grade 1 Math Challenge
Problem 1
Alisa drops 15 acorns and picks up only 11. How many acorns are still on the ground?
Answer: 4
15 − 11 = 4
Problem 2
Maia makes 5 chocolate chip, 7 plain, 6 strawberry, and 4 vanilla cookies. How many cookies did she make in total?
Answer: 22
6 + 4 + 5 + 7 = 22
Problem 3
How many pennies equal one dime?
Answer: 10
Penny = 1, Dime = 10. The answer is ten.
Problem 4
Natalia sees 4 birds, 2 butterflies, and 6 dogs. How many animals does Natalia see?
Answer: 12
4 + 2 + 6 = 12
Problem 5
How many dogs equal one lion?
Answer: 5
One penguin equals 2 dogs. 2 + 2 + 1 = 5
