Week 2 — Answers & Solutions

Grade 1 Math Challenge
Problem 1
How many triangles are there?
Answer: 7
First count the small triangles — there are 3:Then count the big triangles — there are 4:Altogether that's 3 + 4 = 7 triangles.
Problem 2
How many cats are hidden behind the fence?
Answer: 3
Each cat has 4 legs. Count the legs under the fence and group them in fours — there are 3 groups of 4, so 3 cats.
Problem 3
What goes in place of the question mark?
Answer: D
In the top row the animals have big eyes and upside-down (sad) mouths. The missing animal must match that, so the answer is D.
Problem 4
What number did Mira write?
Answer: 10
The smallest one-digit number greater than zero is 1, and the biggest one-digit number is 9. So Mira writes 1 + 9 = 10.
Problem 5
Who has the most lego pieces?
Answer: Coco
Put them on a “more / less” line. The first clue — Bluey has more than Bingo — puts Bluey above Bingo:The second clue — Bluey has fewer than Coco — puts Coco at the very top:So Coco has the most lego pieces.
